Top three activities to do at Krasnogorsk

Gubailovo estate

Construction of the estate began in the 17th century. Znamenskoye-Gubailovo belonged to many owners, but its real heyday began in the early 20th century under its last owner, entrepreneur and patron Alexander Polyakov. Writers, poets and artists of the Silver Age came here: Ivan Bunin, Zinaida Gippius, Konstantin Balmont, Andrei Bely.

Park - Ivanovskie prudy

A 12-minute walk west of the estate brings you to two picturesque lakes with modern embankments, coffee shops, benches, and a workout area.

Victory Museum

An interesting exhibition is housed in a modest two-storey building. For example, you can learn about the history of the reception centre for German prisoners of war, which was located near Krasnogorsk.
